Rajat Kumar

I'm working as an Assistant Professor in the CSE Department at MNIT Jaipur since January 2024. Before that I was an Institute Post Doctoral Fellow at IIT Madras. I received my Ph.D. from the CSE Department at IIT Jammu advised by Dr. Vinit Jakhetiya in January 2023. I primarily work in the area of Image Processing, Computer Vision, and Machine Learning. My main focus includes analyzing and enhancing the perceptual quality of super-resolved images/videos, and 3D synthesized views.

I completed my M.Tech. from SLIET Longowal in 2018 and B.Tech from HPTU in 2016.
